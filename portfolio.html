Ethics

Space Ethics

I work as a Hackworth Fellow supervised by Dr. Brian P. Green at Markkula Center for Applied Ethics.
This project explores the ethical dimensions of the space industry and exploration along with sharing my views with a wider community. Including but not limited to satellites & space debris, international relations in space, and ethical use of space resources, etc

Control System and Robotics

HERMES

In a team of three undergrad ECE students, we implemented a guided tour with a socially assistive robot (NAO 6) using control mechanism and open computer vision.
Supervised by Dr. Maria Kyrarini
Published on SCU Scholar Commons
